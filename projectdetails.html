Studio

British Petroleum

Location: Pune

Project Size: 1,00,000 sq.ft

Year: 2021

A Resimercial workplace, the office design is inspired from the concept of creating a"non - office". Elements from residential design are incorporated to establish the ”coziness of a home “ and make the users of the space feel the warmth and comfort of a residence. The office spans 3 floors and is segregated into neighbourhoods, each which are sustainable with their own meeting , collaborative and workspaces, thus providing equal access to users in different teams.

Sustainability in terms of incorporating sunlight , usage of natural materials such has clay bricks , and installation of locally handcrafted sarees and rugs as decorative elements establish a connect of local flavour and identity to the workplace. The spatial and functional concept between a "traditional office space" and a "comfortable home" have found a perfect symbiotic balance in this resimercial design.
